Our Work

Projects that transform
digital operations

Explore a selection of internal tools, integrated automations and custom-built web platforms.

Digital Agents

Sofi

Sofi is a multichannel conversational automation system that centralizes and optimizes 24/7 digital customer service on WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook and Web, integrating official Meta APIs, n8n flows and artificial intelligence to reduce operational load and improve customer experience.

Leo

Leo is a sales-oriented conversational automation system that organizes and confirms orders on WhatsApp and Instagram, integrating official Meta APIs, n8n flows and intelligent logic to automatically structure orders and optimize commercial processes.

Management Systems

Order & Production Management System

Premium platform for food businesses that centralizes order, stock and production management through a dynamic catalog connected to a central control panel with differentiated roles: Admin Dashboard, Kitchen Panel and real-time Dispatch View.

Rebels Kite School

Web management system developed for surf and kite schools, designed to organize classes, instructors, students, payments and billing through an interactive calendar, status control and role-differentiated panels.

Strategic Websites

Inferno Growshop

Online catalog website designed to showcase products and accept orders via a WhatsApp-integrated cart, offering a lightweight and direct solution for businesses that don't need a traditional e-commerce platform.

Eva (Artistic Portfolio)

Minimalist, responsive web portfolio developed for a visual artist, focused on storytelling, image prominence and simple navigation to showcase her work clearly and aesthetically.
